**TITLE:** Career-Ops

**LINK:** https://github.com/santifer/career-ops

**CATEGORY:** tools

**SUMMARY:** Career-Ops is an open-source, multi-agent job search system built on Claude Code that automates job discovery, evaluation, and CV tailoring. It uses Playwright to scan career portals (Greenhouse, Ashby, Lever), scores job fits across 10 weighted dimensions (A-F grading), and generates ATS-optimized, personalized CVs as PDFs — all running locally on your machine. The creator used it to evaluate 740+ listings, generate 100+ tailored CVs, and land a Head of Applied AI role.

**KEY TAKEAWAYS:**
- **Multi-agent architecture** — 14 skill modes with batch processing that evaluates 10+ offers in parallel using sub-agents
- **10-Dimension scoring system** — structured A-F scoring across weighted dimensions to filter hundreds of listings down to the ones worth applying to
- **Automated portal scanning** — uses Playwright (HITL - Human-in-the-Loop) to navigate Greenhouse, Ashby, Lever, and company career pages
- **ATS-optimized CV generation** — creates tailored PDFs customized per job description, not generic keyword stuffing
- **Privacy-first & local** — fully open-source, runs locally; your CV and personal data never leave your machine (sent only to your chosen AI provider)
- **Human always decides** — the system evaluates and recommends but never auto-submits applications
- **Go dashboard** — provides a visual interface for managing your job search pipeline
